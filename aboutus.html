ABOUT US

StudyVerse is a student run academic platform that makes it easier to stay on track when life gets busy.

StudyVerse allows you to access student-shared resources and contribute your own to help others in the same boat.

Whether you have missed a class, took improper notes, need extra notes

Our aim is to create a collaborative academic space where students can share notes, build flashcards, connect with peers and ask course-specific questions in a way that’s organized, accessible and stress free.

We believe no student should fall behind for reasons beyond their control.

StudyVerse makes sure everyone has the tools they need to succeed and a community to succeed with.

For when you’re overwhelmed, sick or just can’t keep up, StudyVerse has got you covered.
